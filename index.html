Forest Pals

How to Play

Raccoon is looking for all his friends in the forest. But they are hiding! It is your job to help him find them.

  1. Flip over two cards each turn.
  2. If both cards show the same animal, great! You score a point.
  3. If they are different animals, they will turn back face down again.
  4. Be quick! There is a timer that keeps getting faster as you go through the level.
  5. Find all the pairs before time runs out to win the game!
